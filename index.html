About Us

Sukh Suvidha Micro Care Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization working towards holistic community development and sustainable progress. Our mission is to bring positive change in the lives of underprivileged, marginalized and rural communities through education, empowerment and sustainable livelihood programs.
